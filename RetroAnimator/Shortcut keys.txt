-WASD : move selected part(s) or colrect by 1px. If no part or colrect is selected it moves the whole frame.
-SHIFT+WASD : move selected part(s) or colrect by 10px. If no part or colrect is selected it moves the whole frame.

-CTRL+Z : Undo.
-CTRL+SHIFT+Z or CTRL+Y : Redo.
-CTRL+X/C/V : cut/copy/paste part(s) to other frames.

-Delete : will delete the selected part(s) from the Sprites list.

-LMB click on part or colrect : Select part or colrect.
-LMB click on blank canvas : Deselect part(s) or colrect.
-LMB click, hold & drag on blank canvas : Drag the canvas around.
-RMB click & drag on blank canvas : Drag rectangle to multiselect parts.   
-CTRL+LMB click on part : Add to selected parts.
-SHIFT+LMB click on part : Add to selected parts and all in between (list based).
-Mouse wheel on canvas : zoom in and out.

-RMB click on blank canvas, frame, part, animation or timeline frame : opens context popup menus with extra options.

-X : toggle 'X-flip' for selected part(s).
-C : toggle 'White' for selected part(s).

-Space : starts/stops animation

-UP/DOWN cursor keys : select previous/next frame in the frames list (OR animations list if last selected)
-LEFT/RIGHT cursor keys OR 1/2 : select previous/next frame in the animation timeline
